Mozzeralla Stuffed Chicken

Chicken stuffed with mozzeralla, arguably the best cheese in existence topped with an marinira sauce. Hard to overcook due to the marinira sauce keeping most of the mouisture in the chicken.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicked
  • Panko crumbs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit. Get a skillet and oil it.
  • Prepare 3 seperate dishes. One with 1 cup of all purpose flour. One with 2 whisked eggs. Third one with 1 cup on panko bread crumbs, 1/4 cup of parm cheese, and whatever seasoning you'd like.
  • Cut deep pockets into the chicked and fill them with mozzarella. Then press the edges to seal the chicken.
  • Put each chicken breast in flour, then beaten egss, then the breadcrumb mixture.
  • Put each chicken breast in the skillet and fry each side of the chicken for 4-5 minutes. The chicken does not need to be cooked at this point.
  • Get a pan and put you chicken breats in the pan and pour an adequate amount of marinira in the pan.
  • Cook the chicken in the oven for 20 minutes or till the chicken reaches interal temperature of 165 degrees fahrenheit.
